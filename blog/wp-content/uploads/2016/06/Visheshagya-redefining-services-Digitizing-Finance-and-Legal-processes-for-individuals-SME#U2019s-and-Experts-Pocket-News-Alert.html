Visheshagya- redefining services, Digitizing Finance and Legal processes for individuals, SME’s and Experts

Posted by: Napson Fernandes


•Visheshagya is a new e-trade gateway for CA, CMA, CS and Lawyers

May 13,  14.05 PM IST || Pocket News Alert

14th May 2016, New Delhi: Visheshagya has launched their online web portal that aggregates the best and qualified CA, CS and Lawyers. This platform consists of verified specialists, accessible through sound and video consultation wherein one doesn’t have to wait for the physical meetings. The online appointment bookings cater to the time management aspect in today’s hectic and fast paced lives. It’s ONLINE DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT attribute is a major highlight for both clients and experts. Especially for the small and medium size experts and company’s which have not yet moved to digitization of records unlike the multi billionaire companies. Besides enabling frequent usage and easy access on the go, this will help keep your records safe and secure, even in case of natural calamities.

Delays in filling returns are very frequent phenomenon with start ups and small firms. They lack internal accounts and legal departments to look into these processes in a routine.  Besides, important tasks such as Marriage registration, filling of Form 16, tax planning for the year also get delayed due to lack of time and complications involved in the processes involved for the common man. Visheshagya is an ideal platform for such requirements as it facilitates virtual consultation from verified experts and gets your work done at convenience without you having to actually visit your CA or lawyer multiple times.


CA’s and Lawyers often stagnate when it comes to their geographical reach, Visheshagya offers them a platform that they can leverage to generate leads, increase their clientele and a global reach. Payment security comes as an added benefit. Visheshagya addresses the issue by integrating a payment gateway through which clients can pay up while booking appointments, also having an adequate mechanism to refund in case the meeting gets cancelled.

 CA Rashmi Khetrapal, CEO, Visheshagya, Services Redefined says, "After spending 16 years in the field of accounting and finance I could understand what difficulties experts and companies face in the existing scheme of affairs. While filling processes have been digitized by the government, small firms and standalone experts are still struggling to keep pace with this digitized world. Visheshagya facilitates this transformation, making it simpler, more profitable and convenient for both clients and experts”.

Visheshagya also provides a platform for international companies planning to establish themselves in India and require legal and accounting support to understand the Indian legal landscape. So say goodbye to your customary ringing framework; CONNECT with Visheshagya, ACQUIRE hassle free services/ customers and GROW together.
